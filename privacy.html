ReturnKeeper Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-08

English

ReturnKeeper stores return item names, categories, store/order notes, carrier or drop-off notes, expected refund amounts, purchase dates, return-by dates, reminder lead times, receipt notes, proof notes, completion counts, missed counts, and recent activity on your device and in the App Group container used by widgets.

Notification permission is optional and used only for local reminders. Reminders access is optional and used only after you choose to sync dated return deadlines to Apple Reminders.

Free users may see clearly labeled Google AdMob native ads. Plus subscribers stay ad-free. Depending on your ATT choice and device settings, Google may process ad identifiers, device information, and ad interaction data to deliver and measure ads. ReturnKeeper does not create accounts, use cloud sync, connect to retailer or carrier accounts, import email, create returns automatically, guarantee refunds, buy shipping, or handle disputes.

通知权限是可选的，只用于本地提醒。提醒事项权限也是可选的，仅在你选择将带日期的退货截止日同步到 Apple 提醒事项后使用。

免费用户可能看到明确标注的 Google AdMob 原生广告。Plus 订阅用户无广告。根据你的 ATT 选择和设备设置，Google 可能处理广告标识符、设备信息和广告互动数据以投放和衡量广告。ReturnKeeper 不创建账号、不使用云同步、不连接零售商或承运方账号、不导入邮件、不自动创建退货、不保证退款、不购买运单，也不处理争议流程。
