Reminder Boundary

ReturnKeeper uses local notifications and optional Apple Reminders sync after you turn them on. It does not write to Calendar, iPhone Clock, or system alarms, and it does not connect to retailer accounts, carrier accounts, email inboxes, payment accounts, or refund systems.
